Career Path Suggestions

Selecting the right stream is the first step toward a meaningful career journey.

🔬 Science Stream

  • Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Math
  • Courses: B.Tech, MBBS, B.Sc
  • Exams: JEE, NEET, CUET
  • Careers: Engineer, Doctor, Scientist, Researcher

📈 Commerce Stream

  • Subjects: Accountancy, Economics, Business Studies
  • Courses: B.Com, BBA, CA, CS
  • Exams: CUET, CPT, IPMAT
  • Careers: CA, Entrepreneur, Business Analyst

📚 Humanities Stream

  • Subjects: History, Political Science, Psychology
  • Courses: BA, BJMC, BSW
  • Exams: CUET, CLAT, UPSC
  • Careers: Civil Services, Journalist, Social Worker

⚙️ Vocational Stream

  • Subjects: IT, Fashion, Agriculture, Retail
  • Courses: Diploma, Skill-Based Certifications
  • Exams: Skill India, Sectoral Assessments
  • Careers: Designer, Technician, Skilled Trades

🌐 Other / Self-Learning

  • Explore interests through online platforms
  • Courses: MOOCs, Workshops, Internships
  • Examples: Graphic Design, Animation, Content Creation
  • Careers: Artist, Youtuber, Game Designer
