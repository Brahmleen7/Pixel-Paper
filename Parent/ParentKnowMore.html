Parent – Real-Time Monitoring & Engagement

The Parent Login Page ensures that parents stay informed and involved in their child’s academic journey. Upon login, parents are presented with an overview dashboard showing academic performance, attendance, assignment history, and fee payment status—all updated in real-time.

Notable features:
- Instant access to student performance reports
- Alerts for upcoming tests, fees, and pending assignments
- Transparent view of teacher qualifications and student-teacher interactions
- School comparison tools to evaluate institutions based on curriculum, affordability, and academic history

This page bridges communication gaps between parents and educators, fostering trust and encouraging collaborative involvement. It empowers parents to make informed decisions and better support their child’s education.
