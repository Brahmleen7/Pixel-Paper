Teacher & Counselor – Mentorship and Insight-Driven Teaching

The Teacher Login Page transforms educators into mentors by providing powerful tools for performance tracking and individualized support. Teachers can post assignments, upload study materials, evaluate submissions, and monitor student progress—all in one place.

Highlights:
- Visual analytics of student progress and emotional well-being
- Assignment posting, grading, and feedback systems
- Dashboards for identifying learning gaps and offering mentorship
- Counselors can schedule and record sessions aligned with student goals

This login page turns generic teaching into customized mentorship. It reduces repetitive tasks through automation, freeing up time for deeper engagement with students and data-backed decision-making.
